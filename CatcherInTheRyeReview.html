Review for Catcher in the Rye

Author: J.D. Salinger

Catcher in the Rye takes place in the late 1940's and early 1950's and is set on the American east coast. The novel's main character, Holden Caulfield, begins his journey after being expelled from the boy's school Pencey prep.
